Tribute to Anne Hathaway

Anne Hathaway is an American actress known for her roles in films such as "The Devil Wears Prada," "Les Misérables," and "The Princess Diaries." She was born on November 12, 1982, in Brooklyn, New York. Hathaway began her acting career in stage productions before transitioning to television and film. She has received numerous accolades, including an Academy Award, a Golden Globe, and a Primetime Emmy Award. Hathaway is also known for her advocacy for various social and environmental causes.

Achievements

  1. Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role in "Les Misérables" in 2012
  2. Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie Actress Comedy for "The Princess Diaries" in 2001
  3. BAFTA Britannia Award for British Artist of the Year in 2018
